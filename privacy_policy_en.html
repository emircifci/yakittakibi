This Privacy Policy describes our privacy practices regarding the Fuel Tracker mobile application ("App"). By using this app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Important: Our app securely protects your data and only collects necessary information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: Required for app registration
  • Username/ID: To identify your account

1.2 User Content

  • Vehicle Information: Brand, model, license plate, fuel type
  • Fuel Records: Liters, price, date, total cost
  • Photos: Vehicle photos and fuel receipts (optional)
  • Odometer Information: Vehicle mileage readings

1.3 Usage Data

  • App Usage: Which features are used
  • Performance Data: App performance and errors

2. How We Use Your Data

2.1 App Functionality

Collected data is used to provide core app features:

  • Account creation and management
  • Storing and displaying fuel records
  • Creating statistics and reports
  • Data backup and synchronization

2.2 Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: In-app advertisement display
  • Data: Device ID, approximate location, advertising ID
  • Tracking: Used for advertising purposes
  • Personal Info: Email or personal info NOT used for ads

Supabase

  • Purpose: Data storage and authentication
  • Data: Email, vehicle info, fuel records
  • Security: SSL/TLS encrypted secure connection

2.3 Analytics and Improvement

Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve app performance and user experience. This data cannot personally identify you and is used only for app improvements.

3. Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted over SSL/TLS encrypted connections
  • Secure Storage: Secure cloud storage via Supabase platform
  • Authentication: Strong authentication system
  • Data Access: Only you can access your data

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Third-Party Sharing

Your data is shared only with third parties necessary for the app to function:

  • Google AdMob: For advertisement display only
  • Supabase: For data storage

4.2 Legal Situations

Your information may be shared due to legal obligations (court order, legal inquiry, etc.).

5. User Rights

Your rights regarding your data:

5.1 Right to Access

You can access and view all your data within the app.

5.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct incorrect or incomplete information within the app.

5.3 Right to Deletion

You can request permanent deletion of all your data by deleting your account. After account deletion, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

5.4 Data Portability

You can export your data in CSV or JSON format. You can perform this operation from the "Data Backup" section in the app.

5.5 Right to Object

You have the right to object to our data processing activities. You can contact us using our contact information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete this information.

7. Cookies and Tracking

The app stores identifiers and data on your device. This information is necessary for the app to perform its core functions.

8. Privacy Policy Changes
